Brief History

Champy Meal was all started in my incomplete subject, Itew1 is the e-commerce subject when I taking it in the year 2020. My prof gave me an assignment so I can complete the missing requirements.

“It is happy to found out that I can cook and taste it using only a nose, smelling is the important in cooking. Every time when my Mom and dad told me to cook, I easily recognize if it is good taste or not using only a sense of smelling. I realize that when I was 20 years old it is year 2020 the pandemic year. Always remember I told to my parents to cook Champorado. Since Champorado is my favorite, I always vision it to make that valuable included in the business. Because in this age I haven’t seen anyone selling champorado, example. Jollibee has spaghetti, Manginasal has Palabok, Chao King has Lugaw. I didn’t know in KFC and McDo cause I didn’t experience to eat a lot there. So this is the climax of my vision, putting Fried chicken on it and a Gravy with rice in a plate.

Champorado has Fried Chicken, Rice and a Gravy, I don’t know why always pop up in my mind that Meal. Actually I didn’t expect it I just want to eat Champorado.” - Founder: Dela Rosa, Geia M.

Product Description

“Champy” means (Shop) and “Meal” means (the normal meal). Usually Filipino ate Champorado in the Morning or make it snacks even in a cold weather. So Champy meal plan to make new taste of people that will promote it by Filipino. So Champorado was made by Sticky Rice and add some water and Cocoa. Champy add some twist to make it unique. To make custoners more like to eat, it has other dish not only Champorado but Champorado is still the top product in the business.
